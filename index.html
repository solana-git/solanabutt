Nigbutt Tokenomics

Nigbutt Tokenomics is the revolutionary economic framework powering the Niggerbutt decentralized ecosystem. Designed with simplicity, sustainability, and inclusivity at its core, the tokenomics ensures that every participant has a fair chance to benefit from the system.

Supply: Niggerbutt tokens have a capped total supply of 69,000,000 tokens, ensuring scarcity and maintaining long-term value. Initial distribution allocates 50% of tokens to early supporters, 30% to ecosystem development, and 20% to liquidity reserves.

Staking: Token holders can stake Niggerbutt to earn passive rewards. Staking also contributes to network security, incentivizing holders to actively participate in the ecosystem.

Burn Mechanism: A portion of every transaction is automatically burned, reducing the circulating supply over time and increasing the value for long-term holders.

Utility: Niggerbutt tokens can be used for governance voting, accessing premium features in the Nigbutt platform, and trading within the ecosystem.
